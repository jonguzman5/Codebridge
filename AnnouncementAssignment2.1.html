The Walking Dead

Season Premiere: October 23, 2016

Developed by Frank Darabont

Genre:

Serial Drama, Horror, and Zombie Apocalypse



Based on the comic book series by

Robert Kirkman, Tony Moore, and Charlie Adlard

Synopsis

In a post-apocalyptic world filled with the undead (known as walkers) and
opposing groups of survivors (often dangerous), the protagonist Rick Grimes
and his group struggle to survive and adapt.

  • Starring
  • Andrew Lincoln as Rick Grimes
  • Norman Reedus as Daryl Dixon
  • Danai Gurira as Michonne
  • Melissa McBride as Carol Peletier
  • Chandler Riggs as Carl Grimes
  • Lauren Cohan as Maggie Greene
  • Steven Yeun as Glenn Rhee
  • Lennie James as Morgan Jones
